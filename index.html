🎓 Digital Foundations Pro – Summer Computer Literacy Program

💻 For After-12th Students | Duration: 2 Months | Fee: ₹5000

📌 Course Overview

A comprehensive beginner-level course to build a solid foundation in computer usage, productivity software, internet safety, and basic coding skills. Delivered in an engaging, hands-on format ideal for students preparing for college, jobs, or entrepreneurship.

📅 Weekly Schedule Outline

Week Module Title Key Skills Covered Project/Activity
1 Introduction to Computers Hardware/Software, Types of Computers Diagram: Label parts of a computer
2 Operating System & File Management Windows basics, File handling, USB/Cloud Storage Folder structure creation activity
3 Typing & Keyboarding Practice Home row keys, posture, typing software Typing speed tracking + leaderboard
4 Word Processing (MS Word/Google Docs) Formatting, tables, resume writing Resume or report formatting project
5 Spreadsheets (MS Excel/Google Sheets) Data entry, formulas, graphs Budget sheet + pie chart
6 Presentations (PowerPoint/Slides) Slide design, transitions, animations Group presentation
7 Internet, Email & Cyber Safety Safe browsing, email, cyber threats Create & send email with attachment
8 Introduction to Coding (Scratch) Visual programming, logic blocks Mini animated game or story project

🧰 Tools Used

  • Windows PC or Chromebook
  • Google Workspace (Docs, Sheets, Slides)
  • TypingClub, Scratch by MIT
  • Email (Gmail or similar)

📜 Certificate Criteria

  • ✔ Attend 90% of classes
  • ✔ Submit final PowerPoint & Scratch project
  • ✔ Pass final quiz
  • ✔ Complete typing test

🎁 Bonus Add-ons

  • Career counseling session
  • Interview prep tips
  • Intro to freelancing in tech

🌐 Our Story

How a Dream Turned into a Digital Learning Revolution

TechNexus Learning started with a bold belief: Quality tech education should be affordable and accessible to all. In a world driven by innovation, digital skills aren’t optional—they’re essential.

This vision took root in 2022, when our founder struggled to find affordable, real-world tech training. Inspired by shared challenges among students, TechNexus officially launched in April 2025 as a dream-turned-reality.

From a humble home setup to a thriving online academy, we now offer: Python, Ethical Hacking, Robotics, Full Stack, Digital Marketing, and more. And we're just getting started.

We also empower college students with part-time trainer roles—so they gain valuable experience while helping others grow. Because here at TechNexus, we grow together.

Our dream is big:

  • 🏢 A vibrant physical campus full of creativity and collaboration
  • 👩🏽‍🏫 A large, diverse team of professionals & student mentors
  • 🌍 A nationwide and global impact in next-gen tech education

This is just the beginning. Join us in reshaping the future of learning.
